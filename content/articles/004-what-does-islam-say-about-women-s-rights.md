Islam **elevated the status of women** at a time when they had virtually no rights in the world.

Over 1,400 years ago, Islam granted women:

- **The right to own property** and conduct business independently
- **The right to inheritance** (explicitly stated in the Quran)
- **The right to education** — the Prophet ﷺ said: *"Seeking knowledge is an obligation upon every Muslim"* (male and female)
- **The right to choose their spouse** and to refuse a marriage
- **The right to divorce** (known as khul')
- **The right to keep their own earnings** and name after marriage

The Quran says:

> *"And for women are rights over men similar to those of men over women."* — Quran 2:228

The Prophet ﷺ said:

> *"The best of you are those who are best to their women."* — Sunan al-Tirmidhi

Cultural practices in some Muslim-majority countries that oppress women are **contradictions of Islamic teachings**, not reflections of them.
