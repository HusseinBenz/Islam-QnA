Prophet Muhammad ﷺ (peace be upon him) is the **final messenger of God**, sent to all of humanity. He was born in Makkah (modern-day Saudi Arabia) around 570 CE.

Key facts about the Prophet ﷺ:

- He was known as **"Al-Amin" (The Trustworthy)** even before his prophethood
- He received the first revelation from God through Angel Gabriel at age 40
- He preached the message of monotheism (Tawheed) for 23 years
- He was illiterate, yet delivered the Quran — a literary masterpiece that remains unmatched
- He established a just society based on equality, mercy, and rule of law
- He freed slaves, fed the poor, and stood up for the oppressed

The Prophet ﷺ said:

> *"I was sent to perfect good character."* — Al-Muwatta

Even non-Muslim historians like Michael Hart ranked him as the **most influential person in history** for his lasting impact on civilization, law, ethics, and human rights.
