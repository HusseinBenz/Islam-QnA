If you're interested in learning about Islam, here are some excellent starting points:

**Read:**
- The Quran — Start with a good English translation (e.g., Sahih International, or The Clear Quran by Dr. Mustafa Khattab)
- "The Sealed Nectar" (Ar-Raheeq Al-Makhtum) — A biography of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ

**Watch:**
- Lectures by scholars like Mufti Menk, Omar Suleiman, or Nouman Ali Khan on YouTube

**Visit:**
- Your local mosque — most mosques welcome visitors and offer introductory sessions
- Talk to a Muslim friend or neighbor

**Online Resources:**
- [Quran.com](https://quran.com) — Read the Quran with translations
- [Sunnah.com](https://sunnah.com) — Browse authentic Hadith collections

The most important step is to approach with an **open heart and sincere curiosity**. Ask God to guide you to the truth — that's a prayer He always answers.

> *"And those who strive for Us — We will surely guide them to Our ways."* — Quran 29:69
