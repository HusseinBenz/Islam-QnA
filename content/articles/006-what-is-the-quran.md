The Quran is the **holy book of Islam**, believed by Muslims to be the literal word of God (Allah) revealed to Prophet Muhammad ﷺ through Angel Gabriel over a period of 23 years.

Key characteristics of the Quran:

- It has been **perfectly preserved** since its revelation — not a single letter has changed in over 1,400 years
- It was memorized in its entirety by thousands during the Prophet's lifetime and continues to be memorized by millions today
- It contains guidance on **faith, worship, law, ethics, science, and the purpose of life**
- It challenges humanity to produce even a single chapter like it — a challenge that remains unmet

> *"Indeed, it is We who sent down the Quran and indeed, We will be its guardian."* — Quran 15:9

The Quran is not just a religious text — it is a complete guide for living a meaningful, just, and spiritually fulfilling life.
