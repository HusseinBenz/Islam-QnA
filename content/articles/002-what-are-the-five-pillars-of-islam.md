The Five Pillars of Islam are the foundation of a Muslim's life. They are:

1. **Shahada (Declaration of Faith)** — Testifying that there is no god but Allah and that Muhammad is His messenger.
2. **Salah (Prayer)** — Performing the five daily prayers at their prescribed times.
3. **Zakat (Charity)** — Giving 2.5% of one's savings annually to those in need.
4. **Sawm (Fasting)** — Fasting during the month of Ramadan from dawn to sunset.
5. **Hajj (Pilgrimage)** — Making the pilgrimage to Makkah at least once in a lifetime, if physically and financially able.

These pillars represent the core acts of worship and are obligatory for every Muslim who is able.
