Islam is an Arabic word meaning **submission** and **peace**. It is the religion of submitting one's will to the One True God (Allah in Arabic). Islam is not named after a person or a tribe — it is named after the very act of devotion to God.

Islam teaches that there is only **one God**, who has no partners, no sons, and no equals. He is the Creator of the heavens and the earth, and everything in between.

> *"Say: He is Allah, the One. Allah, the Eternal Refuge. He neither begets nor is born. Nor is there to Him any equivalent."* — Quran 112:1-4

Muslims believe in all the prophets sent by God — including Adam, Noah, Abraham, Moses, Jesus, and Muhammad (peace be upon them all). Muhammad ﷺ is the final messenger, and the Quran is the final revelation.
